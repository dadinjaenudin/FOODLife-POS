üìä What is a Store Session?

Store Session represents a single business day or operational period at the store:

  • Opened: When the store opens for business (manual or automatic)
  • Business Date: The accounting date for all transactions in this session
  • Closed: When End-of-Day (EOD) is performed
  • Contains: All bills, payments, cash movements for that day

Note: In Phase 1, sessions are auto-created. Full session management coming in Phase 2.
